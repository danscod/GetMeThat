What is GetMeThat?



Community

GetMeThat is a global marketplace for rare, foreign or just plain hard to get goods. It is buyer driven - buyers place listings of items that they wish to buy, and there listings are responded to b sellers from around the world, who are able and willing to purchase (or otherwise own) said item and are willing to sell it to the Buyer.


What's Special about GetMeThat?



Some Features


First Class Design

Optimised for mobile devices and interfacing with social media, GetMeThat reflects the changing needs of an increasingly advanced generation of online shoppers.


Excellent Value for Money

Through our partnerships with Dwolla and Paypal, GetMeThat is able to offer users significantly lower fees, both on the transfer of funds and our own commission. We keep costs low through cloud-hosted content and a commitment to focus on core business functions.


Global Network

Dedicated to ensuring easy and affordable trade across the world, the team here at GetMeThat endeavour to continue building preferential shipping deals with foreign transporters. In addition, we will seek to engage with expatriate and foreign student communities, as well as the public at large to build long-lasting partnerships to the benefit of the global community.
